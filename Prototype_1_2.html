BackGround

BIOMOD is one of the most popular Molecular Robotics Competition. (BIOMOD Official Website) We were fascinated by policy of the competition and decided to enter the world.

System of OrigaGel

First of all, DNAorigami is a molecular device which is familiar to BIOMOD. In this competition, we decided to add other elements to DNAorigami. Then, we stood up with an idea that DNAorigami convert its solid structure to liquid structure like gels.
At last, our idea led to the device called "DNA OrigaGel" !!
